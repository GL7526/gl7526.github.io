Taiyaki

Taiyaki is a Japanese street food.

Some examples of fillings for taiyaki are:
  • red bean
  • chocolate
  • custard

Here is what Taiyaki is.
One variation of taiyaki is Bungeo-ppang.

It become popular in Korea and now many things are derived from it. One snack that developed from it is an ice cream waffle sandwich called Samanco by Melona.
